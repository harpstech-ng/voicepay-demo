Help Center

How do I reset my PIN? ▼
Go to Settings > Security > Change PIN. You'll need your current PIN to set a new one. If you forgot it, contact support.
How does voice payment work? ▼
Record your voice phrase during enrollment. For transfers, speak your phrase + amount + recipient. VoicePay verifies your voice biometrics before processing.
What are my transaction limits? ▼
Default: ₦50,000 single, ₦500,000 daily, ₦5,000,000 monthly. You can adjust these in Settings > Transaction Limits.
